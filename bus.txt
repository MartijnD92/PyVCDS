2007 VW Passat internal CAN bus notes
bitrate: 100kbps

Bus traffic generated by pressing the unlock button on the fob
all VWTP traffic appears to be between 0x0 and 0x1 of a given ID.

Sat<->Radio traffic may be DDP? (Display Data Protocol, used in VWs with red dash display)

0x680: Something VWTP, appears to be talking to 0x681
0x681: Seems to respond to 0x680.
0x688: VWTP, talks to 0x689.
0x689: VWTP.
0x6D0: Probably the radio. talks to the sat unit, and gets information.
0x6d1: Satellite Radio module. currently unprovisioned in this vehicle.
